Our Story

A vision born in the valley.

EcoRetreat was founded in 2019 by lifelong conservationists Maya Torres and James Whitfield. After spending years traveling the world studying sustainable hospitality, they discovered this untouched valley and knew they had found home.

What began as a small collection of canvas tents has grown into a world-renowned eco-resort that proves luxury and environmental stewardship are not mutually exclusive. Every structure was designed with a minimal footprint — using reclaimed timber, solar power, and rainwater harvesting systems.

Today, EcoRetreat welcomes guests from around the globe, offering them a chance to experience nature without compromising on comfort.

Sustainability Commitment

Built to last, built to give back.

Our commitment to the planet goes beyond buzzwords. Here's how we turn our values into action every day.

100% Renewable Energy

Our entire resort runs on solar and hydro power. We generate 120% of our energy needs, feeding the surplus back to the local grid.

Water Stewardship

Rainwater harvesting and greywater filtration systems supply 80% of our water needs. Low-flow are standard in suite.

Regenerative Practices

For every guest night, we plant a native tree. Our organic garden supplies 60% of our restaurant's produce year-round and round.
