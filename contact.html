FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is the best time to visit?
Each season offers a unique experience. Spring (March-May) brings wildflowers and mild temperatures. Summer (June-August) is perfect for hiking and stargazing. Fall (September-November) offers stunning foliage and foraging. Winter (December-February) is cozy with snow-dusted landscapes.
How do I get to EcoRetreat?
We are located a 2-hour drive from Alpine City International Airport. We offer complimentary shuttle pickup from the airport and the nearby train station. Detailed directions are provided upon booking.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 14 days before check-in. 50% refund for cancellations 7-13 days prior. No refund for cancellations within 7 days. We recommend travel insurance for peace of mind.
Are pets allowed?
Yes! We are a pet-friendly resort. Select safari tents and eco-villas are designated pet-friendly. A one-time cleaning fee of $75 applies. Please notify us at the time of booking.
What should I pack?
Comfortable hiking shoes, layered clothing (evenings get cool), swimwear, a reusable water bottle, and a sense of adventure. We provide eco-friendly toiletries, robes, and slippers in every accommodation.
