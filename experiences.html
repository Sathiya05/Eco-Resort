Signature Experiences

Beyond the room.

Every experience at EcoRetreat is crafted to deepen your connection with the natural world — guided by local experts who share their passion and knowledge.

Culinary

Foraging & Fire Dining

Join our resident chef and foraging expert on a morning walk through the forest to identify wild edibles — mushrooms, herbs, berries, and edible flowers. Bring your harvest back to the open-air kitchen where you'll learn to prepare a multi-course meal over an open flame. The experience culminates in a long-table dinner under the stars, paired with local wines.

Wellness

Forest Bathing Spa

Inspired by the Japanese practice of shinrin-yoku, our forest bathing experience combines guided meditation with therapeutic spa treatments in an open-air pavilion surrounded by ancient trees. Begin with a mindfulness walk, followed by a wild-harvested botanical massage or facial. The experience ends with a cup of locally foraged tea as you absorb the sounds of the forest.

2 hoursPrivate sessionOrganic products

Adventure

Guided Expeditions

Explore the pristine wilderness surrounding EcoRetreat with our expert local guides. Choose from sunrise summit hikes, gentle waterfall walks, full-day alpine treks, or riverside cycling routes. Our guides share deep knowledge of local ecology, geology, and indigenous history. Picnic lunches featuring local ingredients are included on full-day expeditions.

Wellness

Yoga & Meditation

Start your day with sunrise yoga on our ridge-top platform overlooking the valley. Our certified instructors guide sessions suitable for all levels — from gentle Hatha to dynamic Vinyasa. Evening meditation sessions take place in the forest under the canopy, accompanied by the sounds of nature. Private sessions are available.

Night

Stargazing Experience

With zero light pollution and some of the clearest skies in the region, EcoRetreat is a paradise for stargazers. Our guided astronomy sessions use a high-powered telescope to explore planets, constellations, and deep-sky objects. Cozy blankets, hot chocolate, and a fire pit keep you warm as you journey through the cosmos.

1.5 hoursNightly (weather permitting)Family friendly
